How this works

  1. Find a project you'd like to give your feedback on.
  2. Fill out information about your usage of the product when you click "Get Involved." the more completely you fill out the sign-up survey, the better your chances of being contacted!
  3. You'll receive a confirmation email with the information you entered, and your information will be sent to the project researcher.
  4. A researcher from that project team will contact you with the next steps.
    1. If it looks like you'll be a good fit for the project team, we'll invite you to participate.
    2. If it looks like you're not quite the right fit for the project team, we'll keep your contact information on file to we can contact you about projects where we might think you'll be a good fit in the future.
  5. Your information will be kept on file for projects where we might think you'll be a good fit for feedback in the future.
